Be part of the solution. Our mission is to connect environmentally conscious consumers with businesses that prioritize sustainability, particularly those focusing on recycling and upcycling plastics.

About Us

Relastic is a campaign dedicated to promoting sustainable plastic management through the principles of reduce, reuse, and recycle. We connect consumers with businesses that are making a difference in environmental conservation.

Supporting Sustainable Businesses

Connecting conscious consumers with businesses that prioritize our planet.

Recycling Businesses

These companies collect and process plastic waste, transforming it into raw materials that can be used again. By supporting these businesses, you help close the loop in the plastic lifecycle.

Upcycling Businesses

Upcycling transforms plastic waste into products of higher quality or value than the original. These innovative businesses create beautiful, functional items from what would otherwise be waste.

Sustainable Product Makers

These businesses create products designed with sustainability in mind from the start—using recycled materials, creating recyclable packaging, or offering plastic-free alternatives.

Environmental Impact

Plastic pollution affects virtually every ecosystem on Earth. It threatens wildlife, contributes to climate change, and can even impact human health through microplastics that enter our food and water supplies.

A plastic bottle can take up to 450 years to decompose in the environment, releasing harmful chemicals throughout its breakdown process.

Solutions in Action

Around the world, innovative solutions to plastic pollution are emerging:

Biodegradable Alternatives

New plastic alternatives made from plant materials that can break down naturally in the environment.

Advanced Recycling

Technologies that can process more types of plastic, including previously difficult-to-recycle materials.

Community Cleanup

Initiatives targeting plastic pollution hotspots in rivers, beaches, and urban areas.

Policy Changes

Plastic bag bans, extended producer responsibility programs, and other regulations to reduce plastic waste.
